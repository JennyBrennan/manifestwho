About

Manifestwho is a quiz about UK political manifestos. It's mainly supposed to be fun (for a niche, political kind of fun). It's also a little experiment into how well people can recognise messages from manifestos - we've got a suspicion it's quite hard.

Feedback is very welcome - feel free to reach out on Twitter @manifestwho or send a pull request if that's your thing.

Manifestwho is open source. It was first developed Jenny, with plenty of thanks to: Tom, Tom, Daniel and Nik who played the first version of this live over dinner and frankly came up with the idea; Ben for suggesting collecting party affiliation or voting intention to gain deeper insights.
