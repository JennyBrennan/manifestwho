Findings from GE2019

Between 4th December 2019 and 13th December 2019, there were 423 attempts at the quiz.

Each quiz randomly presents 8 of the 25 available questions. 91% of attempts saw all 8 questions answered.

For the sake of these stats, we're considering each 'attempt' at the quiz a person, but in practice some people may have done the quiz twice, or with friends. For stats on total scores on the quiz, we limit the dataset to the 386 'people' who completed 8 questions. For stats on individual questions we include partially answered quizzes.
